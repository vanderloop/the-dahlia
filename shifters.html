shifters

shifters are humanoid creatures that originate from shinge and don’t have a lot of common traits, besides all having some traits from their animal form once they shift for the first time. they, for all intents and purposes, look human and are the closest thing to “human” that lorest has. their main ability is shapeshifting, which allows them to shift into the animal that they present as at 13. any other abilities they have are connected to the animal they shift into. culture between shifters varies from class to class, but they all have a few common overlaps. all shifters, no matter their stance on anything else, care deeply for animals and the earth, as that’s what they’re connected to to be able to shift. shifters are never given middle names and choose a last name that fits with their animal when they first shift. after your first shift, it’s highly frowned upon to completely shift back to your full human form, as it’s seen as disrespecting your animal and spitting on your ability. no matter how each shifter group feels about the others, there’s always a sense of community between shifters of similar species.
